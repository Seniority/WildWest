## Welcome to Wild West! In this great console shooter, you will duel an opponent for your glory and reputation.
# Each duelist can take up to 5 shots.
# The closer you are to your opponent, the more likely shots will hit.
# Revolvers only hold six bullets! Don't forget to reload. Your cowboy or bandit can load 2 bullets at a time.
